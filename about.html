Our Story

Chris Price Music formed out of a desire to create music that would help ensembles achieve a higher level of musicianship. Although a small team, each member has experience in designing powerful experiences for any size ensemble.

Our Team


Chris Price - Composer/Arranger

Coming from a background in computer science and mathamatics, Chris Price would spend his free time building synthesizers and getting a deeper meaning of how sound and music works. With a fascination of spectral music, Chris would compose with the idea of how the overall sound would be in the music. Chris has written for many ensembles all across the globe, and holds the title of composing the unofficial Antartica anthem of Palmer Station. With several elite marching ensembles under his belt, including 2 Drum Corps International groups, Price can write very memorable music.


Austin Moldenhaur - Visual Designer

Austin Moldenhaur has written and designed drill for several ensembles. Over the years, Austin has had the chance to write drill for the Boise Gems Drum and Bugle Corps, as well as several high schools around the boise area. Austin currently attends Boise State University, where's he's studying to teach music.
